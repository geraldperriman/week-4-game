Crystals Collector



  • You will be shown a random number at the start of the game.



  • There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.



  • You win the game if your total score matches the random number and you loses the game if the total score goes above the random number.



  • The value of the crystal adds is not shown to you until you click on it. but their total score is displayed and updated after each crystal is clicked.



  • When the game is restarted, the player will be shown a new random number, all the crystals will now have 4 different hidden values, and the player's total score is reset to 0.
